# Commissioner Watson

**Role:** Planning Commissioner, Town of Paonia
**Status:** Active as of 2024-12-02

## Significance

Watson is an independent corroborating source for Pete McCarthy's claims about the hostile environment at Town Hall. She was not part of Pete's complaint and appears to have reached similar conclusions independently.

## Key Statements (December 2, 2024 Meeting)

At the Planning Commission meeting where Pete's resignation was read into the record, Watson made several statements on the record:

- Revealed she had been **excluded from email communications** that other commissioners received. [[meeting-2024-12-02|source]]
- **Accused [[stefen-wynn|Wynn]] of slandering her to the Board of Trustees.** [[meeting-2024-12-02|source]]
- Independently corroborated Pete's claims about hostile treatment.
- When told "you are not a lawyer" and "take it to the Board of Trustees," Watson responded: "call the police and arrest me." [[meeting-2024-12-02|source]]

## Why This Matters

Watson's statements are significant because:
1. She is not Pete McCarthy. Her observations are independent.
2. She was a sitting commissioner at the time, not a former one with a grievance.
3. She describes the same pattern Pete documented: exclusion from communications, hostile treatment, dismissal of concerns.
4. Her statement about being slandered to the Board suggests Wynn may have been working to undermine commissioners who asked questions, not just Pete.

## Open Questions

- What specifically did Wynn say about Watson to the Board?
- Was Watson excluded from the same emails that Pete was included on, or different ones?
- Did Watson file any formal concerns of her own?
