# Samira Vetter

**Role:** Town Clerk, Town of Paonia
**Status:** Current (as of 2026-04-05)

## Key Actions

### CORA obstruction
- Denied Pete McCarthy's CORA request for M365 MailItemsAccessed audit logs on the pretext that records were "held by a third party." Those logs would have verified whether Wynn accessed a specific email on 2024-11-12. [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]
- The Town of Paonia has a prior judicial finding of CORA bad faith: Judge Steven Schultz found the town "failed to exercise reasonable diligence or reasonable inquiry" in the Brunner CORA case and ordered the town to pay legal costs. [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]

### Initiative rejections
- Rejected Pete McCarthy's STR citizen initiative on 2025-05-28 under C.R.S. 31-11-106(1). Rejection lacked specificity required by law. [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]
- The Town Attorney's detailed legal analysis existed at the time of rejection but was withheld from the petitioner for nearly ten months. [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]
- Rejected the surveillance camera initiative on 2026-04-03 on six grounds. [[fb-05-initiative-rejected-again|source]] [[2026-04-03-surveillance-initiative-rejection|event]]
- All six grounds were rebutted in a formal written response. [[rejection-response-to-vetter|source]]
- Pete prepared split single-subject initiatives, a revised narrowed petition, and a Rule 106 petition draft for judicial review. [[split-initiatives|source]] [[rule-106-petition-draft|source]]
- Pattern across both rejections: characterize provisions as "administrative" to place them beyond citizen initiative authority. In both cases, the clerk's stated basis mischaracterizes the initiative text.
