## Documented Actions

### Verkada camera usage (2025-10 to 2026-03)

The [[cora-c26-09-verkada-audit-logs|CORA C 26-09 audit log exports]] document that Santiago is the single largest user of the Town's Verkada surveillance system. Across a 6-month logged period:

- **16,653 total events** (51.5% of all user activity in the system)
- **15,509 live stream events** (more than any other user)
- **1,670 live views of the Community Room camera** where public meetings and public comment occur
- **14 Archive Action Taken events** across the Front Desk, Grand Ave., and Community Room cameras

Full breakdown in [[verkada-usage-patterns]].

### Community Room archive sequence (2026-03-20 and 2026-03-21)

Between 2:41 PM and 3:54 PM MDT on Friday 2026-03-20, Santiago created three permanent archives of the Community Room camera. The session included toggling the camera's audio setting three times, scrubbing through historical footage multiple times, and saving three permanent clips. He returned Saturday morning (2026-03-21, 11:51 AM MDT) and created a fourth archive.

The Community Room is Town Hall's meeting space where the Board of Trustees, Planning Commission, and public committees hold meetings and where citizens make public comment. Archive Action Taken events permanently save footage beyond the 30-day retention window.

The associated archive metadata (name, description, case number, tags, time window, sharing history) was not produced in the CORA response. See [[march-20-community-room-archives]].

### Usage pattern

Santiago's Community Room viewing does not align with a meeting-minutes workflow:

- **Sunday is the highest day of the week** (342 views). The Board does not meet on weekends.
- **35% of views are on weekends.**
- **428 views occur after 5 PM** (outside regular office hours).
- **408 views occur before 9 AM** (before regular office hours).
- **93.6% of Community Room events are Live Stream Started** (watching in real time), only 4.8% are Video History Streamed (reviewing past footage).
