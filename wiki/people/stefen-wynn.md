# Stefen Wynn

**Role:** Town Administrator, Town of Paonia
**Status:** Current (as of 2026-04-05)

## Background

Prior to Paonia, Wynn served as City Manager of Neptune Beach, Florida. He was unanimously fired in January 2023 for "flagrant neglect of duty" and "willful misconduct which substantially impairs or damages the interests or reputation of the city." He filed a whistleblower retaliation lawsuit (Case No. 23-CA-8801, Duval County Circuit Court). Jury trial scheduled for the week of 2026-08-17.

Sources: News4Jax (2023-06-27), WOKV (2023-01-18). Court filings in `raw/documents/bowman-case/`.

## Key Actions (Paonia)

### Brunner conflict (2024)
- Submitted a conditional resignation on 2024-08-01, stating he would withdraw it if [[bill-brunner|Trustee Brunner]] was removed. [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]
- Told Pete McCarthy that Brunner had "nefarious ends." [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]

### Email deception (2024-11-04)
- Pete emailed detailed questions to Wynn at 1:13 PM via his official Planning Commission address, requesting inclusion in that evening's meeting packet. [[reporter-package-email-coverup|source]]
- At the Planning Commission meeting that evening (~13:41 on video), Wynn said "Oh, we do have an email from Pete" as if discovering it for the first time. Video available on YouTube.
- The next morning at 1:10 AM, Wynn forwarded the email to the full Board and Planning Commission. Later claimed he "spent a 14-hour day" preparing to address the questions.
- Pete's formal complaint (2024-11-18) specifically requested the Board obtain M365 MailItemsAccessed audit logs for this email exchange. Those logs would show exactly when Wynn opened the email, on which device, and how many times. The Board never obtained them.

### McCarthy conflict (2024)
- Forwarded Pete McCarthy's private emails to all trustees and commissioners with hostile editorial framing on at least three occasions (2024-09-30, 2024-10-31, 2024-11-14). [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]
- Called McCarthy's Planning Commission work "a waste of Town resources" (2024-11-14). [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]
- Dismissed Pete's role: "Your role as a planning commissioner is advisory, not legislative" (2024-11-14). [[reporter-package-email-coverup|source]]
- Threatened to not attend meetings: "I will not attend the December 2, 2024 meeting unless required to by the Board of Trustees." [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]
- When Pete appealed the CORA denial to Wynn: "I'm not the correct person to send a CORA request to... even forwarding this request is not a duty I am obligated to perform and I'm doing this to accommodate you." (2024-11-25) [[reporter-package-email-coverup|source]]

### Bowman termination (2025)
- [[mayor-smith|Mayor Smith]] disclosed [[kaja-bowman|Kaja Bowman]]'s identity to Wynn after Bowman inquired about the complaint process on 2025-09-16. Bowman was terminated 2025-10-09, 23 days later. [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]

### Special meeting response (2026)
