# Mayor Paige Smith

**Role:** Mayor, Town of Paonia (Paige Smith)
**Status:** Current (as of 2026-04-05)

## Key Actions

### Brunner removal (2024-08)
- Brought the charges against [[bill-brunner|Trustee Brunner]] and served as prosecutor at the removal hearing. Could not vote. [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]

### Bowman disclosure (2025-09)
- After [[kaja-bowman|Kaja Bowman]] approached the mayor on 2025-09-16 to ask whether there was a process for making a complaint about the Town Administrator and HR, Mayor Smith contacted [[stefen-wynn|Stefen Wynn]], identified Bowman by name, and characterized the inquiry as a complaint. Bowman had not stated she was making a complaint. Bowman was terminated 23 days later. [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]

### Special meeting (2026-03-30)
- Mayor's agenda report called Pete's 10 Things flyer "libelous." [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]
- Proposed spending taxpayer money on paid display ads in three newspapers (Merchant Herald, High Country Shopper, Delta County Independent) to respond to the flyer. [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]
- Report referred to the "anonymous author(s)" despite the board being aware Pete had publicly claimed the flyer. [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]
- Stated concern that public criticism "can place us in jeopardy with our current granting and lending agencies." [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]

### Retraction demand (2026-03-31)
- Pete sent a formal demand for retraction to Mayor Smith and all board members. [[retraction-demand-2026-03-31|source]]
- Demanded retraction and correction within 10 business days.
- Reserved rights under Colorado law including defamation, Section 1983, and anti-SLAPP.

### Public statements
- Has stated publicly that her most important duty is to "protect the town staff." [[fb-03-legal-analysis-libelous|source]]
- Facebook post #3 calls for her resignation based on the pattern: disclosed Bowman, ignored McCarthy's complaint, ignored Heiniger's accusations, then used taxpayer resources to label citizen criticism "libelous."

## Voting Record
