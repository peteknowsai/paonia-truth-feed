# Lucy Hunter

**Role:** Trustee, Town of Paonia
**Status:** Current (as of 2026-04-05)

## Voting Record

- **2024-08-15**: Voted to remove [[bill-brunner|Trustee Brunner]]. (4-1 vote, for removal) [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]
- **2026-03-30**: Voted to publish the board's response calling Pete's flyer "libelous." (5-1 vote, for publication) [[fb-02-they-didnt-edit-a-word|source]]

## Key Actions

### Counter-flyer (2026-03-26)
- Created a counter-flyer responding to Pete's 10 Things flyer, posted throughout town unsigned within 24 hours of the flyer's distribution. [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]
- Per the mayor's agenda report, the counter-flyer was coordinated with [[stefen-wynn|Town Administrator Wynn]] and [[clayton-buchner|Town Attorney Buchner]]. [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]

### Response to formal complaint (2026-03-31)
- Only board member to respond to Pete's formal complaint letter. [[lucy-hunter-response-2026-03-31|source]]
- Wrote in individual capacity, not on behalf of the Board.
- Acknowledged the complaint and stated she takes its contents seriously.
- Could not address matters involving pending litigation or personnel affairs.
- Agreed that a complaint process should exist: "A municipality should have a clear, documented, and accessible process that ensures complaints of this nature are received by the Board directly, not routed through the subject of the complaint."
- Stated she intends to raise the complaint process as a matter requiring the Board's attention.

### Discrepancy
- Hunter's rebuttal flyer was unsigned, yet the mayor's report treated it as a legitimate, authorized response. Pete's sourced, publicly claimed flyer was characterized as "libelous." [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]
