# "10 Things" Flyer Distributed

**Date:** 2026-03-25
**Type:** Citizen flyer distribution

## What Happened

Pete McCarthy distributed a flyer titled "10 Things Paonia Should Know About Its Town Administrator" throughout town. Every claim cited adopted Town budgets, published news articles, court records, and public meeting minutes. Sources were listed at the bottom. A separate 19-page sourced citation document was compiled.

## Key Claims in the Flyer

All sourced from public records (see [[citation-sheet]] for full source list):

- [[stefen-wynn|Wynn]] fired unanimously from Neptune Beach for "flagrant neglect of duty" and "willful misconduct"
- Wynn holds both Administrator and Treasurer roles
- Fund balances projected to drop 23%
- 69% of budget is external funding
- Staff grew from 19 to 25.7 FTE while population grew 1.1%
- $50,288 Verkada cameras with zero public comment
- Tree Board resigned, budget allocates $0 to dedicated tree care
- Pattern of consequences for people who raise concerns

## Response Timeline

- **2026-03-25**: Flyer distributed
- **2026-03-26**: [[lucy-hunter|Trustee Hunter]] posts unsigned counter-flyer, coordinated with Wynn and [[clayton-buchner|Buchner]]
- **2026-03-30**: Board holds [[2026-03-30-special-meeting|special meeting]] to approve official response. [[mayor-smith|Mayor]]'s report calls flyer "libelous."
- **2026-03-30**: Board votes 5-1 to publish response
- **2026-03-31**: Board publishes response without the edits they voted to make

## Contrast

Pete's formal complaint (2024-11-18) documenting specific statutory violations with dates and quotes: 16 months, zero response from any board member.

Pete's flyer citing the town's own budget: 5 days to special meeting, unified public statement, proposed taxpayer-funded newspaper ads.
