# Special Meeting — Interim Administrator & Search Process

**Date:** 2026-05-22, 6:00 PM
**Type:** Special meeting, Board of Trustees
**Location:** 214 Grand Avenue / Zoom (Meeting ID 838 6717 5450)

> Prospective entry — written the day of the meeting. To be updated with what
> the Board actually decided.

## Why this meeting was called

[[stefen-wynn|Town Administrator Stefen Wynn]]'s three-year contract is not
being renewed. The Town's finance memo dated May 19, 2026 carries the subject
line "Town Administrator terms of nonrenewal" and projects his compensation
running through **mid-July 2026**. The Board called a special meeting to handle
the gap.

This follows the Board's early-May vote to **pause Wynn's reappointment for
30 days**, during which residents publicly questioned whether the combined
Administrator/Treasurer role should continue in its current form.

## Agenda

Single substantive item:

> Discussion and Consideration of Hiring an Interim Administrator and the
> Process for Hiring a Full-Time Administrator

## What the packet proposes

- An **interim administrator** to bridge the transition.
- A **process for the permanent hire** — the packet attaches the City of
  Florence, CO 2022 City Manager search RFP as a template, signaling intent to
  run a conventional **national executive-search** process via a hired
  consulting firm.
- The finance memo names **no cost figure** for the search firm.

See [[admin-search-special-meeting-2026-05-22|the full packet]] for details.

## Citizen response

[[admin-search]] — a citizen proposal published the same day — argues the Board
should (1) disclose the search-firm cost before voting, (2) weigh growing and
training a local hire against a national search, (3) seat residents on the
hiring committee, and (4) use the search as the moment to unbundle the
over-concentrated Administrator/Treasurer role.
