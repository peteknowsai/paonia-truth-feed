# Special Meeting — Interim Administrator Interview & Recall Announcement

**Date:** 2026-05-29 (afternoon)
**Type:** Special meeting, Board of Trustees
**Video:** Town of Paonia, "05/29/2026 Special Board Meeting," YouTube `NFQKdNRzjtw` (CivicClerk event 573)

## Why this meeting was called

To interview a single interim town-administrator candidate, [[greg-sund|Greg Sund]], and consider a consultant contract bridging the departure of [[stefen-wynn|Town Administrator Stefen Wynn]] (leaving mid-July 2026). This followed the [[2026-05-22-special-meeting|May 22 special meeting]] on the nonrenewal and search process.

## What happened

- **One candidate.** Sund was the only name before the Board, sourced through the **CCMA senior advisor**, who reported "no other interim candidates." See [[the-ccma-pipeline]].
- **The interview.** Sund fielded questions on continuity, grant-funded water projects, public works, citizen committees, and HR. He confirmed making the "this is not a democracy" remark to Trinidad's finance director (attributing it to board direction) and described how towns frequently part ways with managers amid board turnover. Full quotes: [[2026-05-29-meeting-transcript]].
- **Public comment.** [[pete-mccarthy-statement-2026-05-29|Pete McCarthy]] read a statement laying out Sund's record and the rushed, single-candidate process, then announced a petition to **recall Mayor [[mayor-smith|Paige Smith]]**, naming himself as the successor candidate.
- **The hire stalled.** A motion to enter the consultant agreement **died for lack of a second.** Trustee Foster objected to hiring within a 24-hour window without reference checks: "cutting corners only sets us up for criticism in the future."

## Outcome

The Board set a **follow-up special meeting for Wednesday, June 3, 2026 at 5:30 p.m.** to complete reference checks, allow contract revisions, and decide. No hire was made on May 29.
