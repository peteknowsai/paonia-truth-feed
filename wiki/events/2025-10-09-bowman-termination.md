# Kaja Bowman Termination

**Date:** 2025-10-09
**Type:** Employee termination

## Timeline

- **2025-09-16**: At the request of several town employees, [[kaja-bowman|Kaja Bowman]] (Deputy Treasurer) approached [[mayor-smith|Mayor Smith]] to ask whether there was a process for making a complaint to the Board involving the Town Administrator and HR. Bowman had not stated she was making a complaint.
- **2025-09-16 (after)**: Mayor Smith contacted [[stefen-wynn|Stefen Wynn]], identified Bowman by name, and characterized the inquiry as a complaint before Bowman ever said it was one.
- **2025-10-09**: Bowman was terminated. 23 days after the inquiry.

## What the Record Does Not Show

- The stated basis for the termination
- Whether the termination was connected to the complaint inquiry
- Whether the Board reviewed or approved the termination
- Whether other employees who requested Bowman make the inquiry faced consequences
