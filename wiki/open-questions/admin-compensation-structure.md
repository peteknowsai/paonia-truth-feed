# What Is the Administrator's Full Compensation?

**Question:** Stefen Wynn holds both the Town Administrator and Town Treasurer roles. What is his total compensation including salary, benefits, any stipend for the Treasurer role, and how does it compare to administrators in comparable towns?

## What the Record Shows

- Wynn holds both Administrator and Treasurer roles (Resolutions 2023-07, 2023-08). [[citation-sheet|source]]
- $106K in admin costs are allocated across four enterprise fund pages. [[citation-sheet|source]]
- The board says "The Administrator does NOT determine his own salary" and trustees conduct performance reviews. [[hunter-rebuttal-flyer|source]]
- FY2026 total personnel costs: approximately $1.7M for 23 FTE, with 2-3% COLA and 2-3% merit raises. (High Country Spotlight)

## What the Record Does Not Show

- Wynn's actual salary and total compensation package
- Whether he receives separate compensation for the Treasurer role
- How the $106K in admin costs across enterprise funds relates to his compensation
- How his compensation compares to administrators in towns of similar size in Colorado
- The terms of his employment contract

## Suggested CORA Requests

**Request 1: Employment Contract**
> Under C.R.S. 24-72-201 et seq., I request a copy of the current employment agreement between the Town of Paonia and Town Administrator Stefen Wynn, including any amendments, addenda, or modifications since the original appointment in July 2023.

**Request 2: Compensation Detail**
> Under C.R.S. 24-72-201 et seq., I request a breakdown of the total compensation for the Town Administrator/Treasurer position for FY2024, FY2025, and FY2026, including: base salary, any additional stipend for the Treasurer role, benefits (health, retirement, vehicle, phone, etc.), and the specific budget line items where compensation is charged.

**Request 3: Admin Cost Allocation**
> Under C.R.S. 24-72-201 et seq., I request documentation showing how the approximately $106,000 in administrative costs referenced in the FY2026 adopted budget is allocated across the four enterprise fund pages, including what services or costs each allocation covers.

## Why This Matters

The Administrator prepares the budget, holds the Treasurer title, and has his compensation spread across multiple fund pages. Citizens are entitled to know the total cost of this dual role in a straightforward, consolidated number, and whether it's in line with comparable positions.
