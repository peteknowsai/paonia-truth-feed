# What Were the Circumstances of Bowman's Termination?

**Question:** What was the stated basis for [[kaja-bowman|Kaja Bowman]]'s termination on 2025-10-09, and was it connected to her inquiry about the complaint process on 2025-09-16?

## What the Record Shows

- Bowman approached [[mayor-smith|Mayor Smith]] on 2025-09-16 at the request of several town employees to ask about the complaint process.
- The mayor contacted [[stefen-wynn|Wynn]], identified Bowman by name, and characterized the inquiry as a complaint.
- Bowman was terminated 23 days later.
- The board complaint letter requested "a review of the circumstances surrounding Ms. Bowman's termination, including the Mayor's role in disclosing the employee's identity to Mr. Wynn."
