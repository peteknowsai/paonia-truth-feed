**Recipient:** Pete McCarthy

## Summary

The only response from any Board member to Pete McCarthy's formal complaint. Hunter writes in her individual capacity, acknowledges the complaint, and agrees that a formal complaint process should exist. She commits to raising the issue with the Board but makes no promises on outcome or timeline.

## Key Facts

- Hunter is the only Board member to respond to the formal complaint
- Writing explicitly in her individual capacity, not on behalf of the full Board
- Acknowledges receipt of the formal complaint and states she takes the contents seriously
- Cannot address matters involving pending litigation or personnel affairs
- Agrees that a formal complaint process should exist for the municipality
- Intends to raise the complaint process issue as a matter requiring the Board's attention
- Does not commit to any specific outcome or timeline
- Thanks Pete for his civic engagement

## Notable Quotes

- "A municipality should have a clear, documented, and accessible process that ensures complaints of this nature are received by the Board directly - not routed through the subject of the complaint"
