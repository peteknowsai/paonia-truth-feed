# Pete McCarthy's Resignation from the Planning Commission (December 2, 2024)

**Raw file:** `raw/documents/board-complaint/resignation-2024-12-02.md`
**Type:** Formal written resignation letter to all Board of Trustees members
**Date:** Monday, December 2, 2024, 12:28:59 PM MST
**From:** Pete McCarthy, Planning Commissioner
**To:** Mayor Paige Smith (PaigeS@townofpaonia.com), Trustee John V. (JohnV@), Trustee Rick Stelter (RickS@), Trustee Kathy Swartz (kswartz@), Trustee Walter Czech (WalterC@), Trustee Lucy Hunter (LucyH@), Trustee Mike Heck (MikeH@)
**Subject:** "Resignation from the Planning Commission"
**Gmail ID:** 19388d8f4d4846b2

## Summary

On December 2, 2024, exactly two weeks after [[complaint-2024-11-18|his formal written complaint to the Board]] went unanswered, Planning Commissioner Pete McCarthy resigned from the Planning Commission effective immediately. The resignation letter was sent to the same seven Board of Trustees members who had received the original complaint. It explicitly cited their silence as one of the stated reasons for the resignation.

**Zero trustees responded to the resignation letter.** Gmail thread records confirm no replies were received from any board member. The resignation was read into the record at the December 2, 2024 Planning Commission meeting later that evening (see [[meeting-2024-12-02]]).

## Full Text

> Dear Board of Trustees,
>
> I hereby resign my position as Planning Commissioner, effective immediately.
>
> Two weeks have passed since I submitted a detailed documentation of serious governance issues to this Board. Your complete silence speaks volumes about the state of oversight in our town.
>
> I take this step only after witnessing the systematic dismantling of Planning Commission authority and experiencing persistent hostility while attempting to fulfill my duties. The November 20th work session transcript clearly documents:
>
> - The Town Administrator threatening to abandon his duties when I asked basic process questions
> - The Mayor and Administrator working in tandem to dismiss legitimate requests for documentation
> - My repeated requests over two months for written policy explanations being stonewalled
> - A pattern of administrative retaliation when commissioners raise concerns
>
> The Planning Commission has been reduced to a ceremonial role, expected to rubber-stamp decisions without proper review or documentation. When commissioners attempt to exercise meaningful oversight, they face hostility and dismissal from town administration.
>
> Your continued silence regarding these serious issues makes clear that proper oversight is not a priority for this board. I cannot in good conscience continue serving on a commission where asking questions and requesting documentation is met with threats and retaliation.
>
> The community deserves better than performative governance. My duty is to the citizens of this town. Since I can no longer effectively fulfill that duty in this compromised environment, I must step aside.
>
> For the record,
> Pete McCarthy
> Former Planning Commissioner

## Key Facts

- **Sent exactly two weeks after the [[complaint-2024-11-18|November 18, 2024 complaint]]**, giving the Board a full two-week window to respond, investigate, or even acknowledge receipt. None occurred.
- **Directly cites Board silence** as a stated reason for resignation: "Your complete silence speaks volumes about the state of oversight in our town."
- **Names specific incidents** from the November 20 work session: Wynn threatening to abandon duties, the Mayor and Administrator working in tandem to dismiss documentation requests, stonewalling of written policy explanations, and a pattern of retaliation against commissioners who raised concerns.
- **Frames the structural problem**: "The Planning Commission has been reduced to a ceremonial role, expected to rubber-stamp decisions without proper review or documentation."
- **Characterizes the Board's non-response as telling**: "Your continued silence regarding these serious issues makes clear that proper oversight is not a priority for this board."
- **Delivered to all seven trustees** via email (same recipient list as the November 18 complaint).

## The Board's Response

**Zero replies.** Not one trustee responded to the resignation letter. The resignation was read into the record at the Planning Commission meeting later that evening ([[meeting-2024-12-02]]). Commissioner Watson independently corroborated the hostile environment at the same meeting, stating that she had been excluded from internal communications and that Wynn had slandered her to the Board.

The pattern established by the [[complaint-2024-11-18|November 18 complaint]] — the Board receiving written documentation of serious concerns and responding with silence — was confirmed by the equally non-responsive treatment of the resignation letter.

## Significance

This letter is the final documented formal communication from Pete McCarthy to the Board of Trustees in his capacity as Planning Commissioner. Along with the [[complaint-2024-11-18|November 18, 2024 complaint]], it constitutes the original pair of written notices that the Board received, did not investigate, and did not respond to.

The pattern the resignation letter described — "administrative retaliation when commissioners raise concerns" — has since been corroborated by [[commissioner-watson|Commissioner Watson]]'s independent testimony, by [[kaja-bowman|Kaja Bowman]]'s written account of her own termination, by [[cory-heiniger|Cory Heiniger]]'s accusations in his resignation letter, and by the documentary evidence produced in [[cora-c26-09-verkada-audit-logs|CORA C 26-09]] showing targeted employee surveillance through the Town's Verkada system ([[employee-surveillance-archives]]).

The resignation letter is cited in the [[board-letter-2026-03-30|March 30, 2026 resubmission letter]] as the second documented instance of the Board's non-response to written concerns about Town Administrator [[stefen-wynn|Stefen Wynn]].
