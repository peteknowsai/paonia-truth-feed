## Finance Memo (packet pp. 5)

**From:** Ashley Rodarte, Payroll and Accounting Specialist
**To:** The Board of Trustees
**Date:** May 19, 2026
**Subject (verbatim):** "Town Administrator terms of nonrenewal"

The Town's own document uses the word **nonrenewal** — not "resignation." [[stefen-wynn|Wynn]] was hired in 2023 on a three-year contract; the memo projects his compensation running through **mid-July 2026**.

## RFP Template (packet pp. 6–8)

The packet attaches a **City of Florence, Colorado** "Request for Proposal for Recruitment Services" (City Manager search, 2022). It is **watermarked "EXAMPLE"** — a template, not a Paonia document. It indicates the Board intends to model its own search RFP on a conventional national executive-search process.

Notable in the Florence template: the hiring committee is "**2 Council and 3 Staff**" — zero citizen seats. See [[admin-search]] for the case that Paonia's committee should include residents.
