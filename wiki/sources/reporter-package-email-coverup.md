# Reporter Package - Email Deception and CORA Cover-Up Timeline

**Raw file:** `raw/documents/media/reporter-package-stefen-email-coverup.pdf`
**Type:** Media package prepared for Delta County Independent
**Date:** April 2026
**Author:** Pete McCarthy

## Summary

A detailed timeline documenting a single thread: [[stefen-wynn|Stefen Wynn]] received an email from a Planning Commissioner hours before a public meeting, then acted on camera as if he had never seen it. When Pete asked the Board to pull the audit logs that would prove it, [[samira-vetter|the Town Clerk]] blocked the records request. When the Colorado Freedom of Information Coalition confirmed the denial was improper, nobody acted.

## Key Emails Referenced

### Email #1: Pete's Questions to Wynn (Nov 4, 2024, 1:13 PM)
Three areas: (1) Procedural authority, citing C.R.S. 31-23-204 on Planning Commission autonomy; (2) Meeting conduct complaints (staff calling Points of Order, Hunter's hostile conduct, staff member Ruben muting Pete mid-sentence); (3) Housing Element objection (28 new undocumented policies, omission of STR regulation which had community's highest-voted priority with 21 votes).

### Email #2: Formal Complaint to Board (Nov 18, 2024)
Requested M365 MailItemsAccessed audit logs that would show: exact time email was sent, when/if it was accessed, through which client/device, and all subsequent access patterns. Also documented: Wynn threatening to skip Dec 2 meeting, bypassing Planning Commission on STR ordinance (violating C.R.S. 31-23-306), commissioners being muted, 28 new housing policies without documentation.

### Email #3: CFOIC Confirms Denial Improper (Nov 25, 2024)
Jeff Roberts confirmed Pete's position was correct and the CORA denial was improper. Suggested requesting the M365 contract with Phonz+ and invoking C.R.S. 24-72-204(4) to demand written grounds for denial.

### Email #4: Wynn Deflects CORA Appeal (Nov 25, 2024)
Wynn: "I'm not the correct person to send a CORA request to... even forwarding this request is not a duty I am obligated to perform and I'm doing this to accommodate you."

### Email #5: Resignation (Dec 2, 2024, 12:28 PM)
References "the systematic dismantling of Planning Commission authority and experiencing persistent hostility." Cites the Nov 20 work session transcript documenting: Wynn threatening to abandon duties when asked basic process questions, Mayor and Administrator working in tandem to dismiss requests for documentation, repeated requests for written policy explanations being stonewalled.
