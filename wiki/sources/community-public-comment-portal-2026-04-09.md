## Key facts

- The Town of Paonia operates an official online public comment portal at:
  **https://townofpaonia.colorado.gov/board-meeting-agendas-and-minutes**
- The portal is the official channel for (1) requesting items be added to a BOT agenda, and (2) creating a documented public record tied to a specific meeting.
- Comments submitted through the portal create a record the Town must reference and that can be cited in subsequent proceedings.
- Ostrander recommends submitting through the portal **and** showing up to voice the same item during the public comment period — this pairs the written record with a verbal one.
