Draft emails sent to five Colorado attorneys with relevant specializations, seeking representation regarding the Board's "libelous" characterization and the broader pattern of retaliation. Each email summarizes the situation and available documentation.

### Common Elements Across All Emails
- Summarize the Board's use of "libelous" to characterize documented, independently reported facts
- Describe a pattern of retaliation against civic engagement
- Note that extensive documentation is available
- Reference the Board's 5-1 vote to publish the statement

### Legal Theories Mentioned
- Defamation (government labeling a citizen's speech as "libelous")
- Defamation per se (the "libelous" label itself)
- Section 1983 (civil rights violation under color of government authority)
- Anti-SLAPP (government using official statements to chill public participation)
