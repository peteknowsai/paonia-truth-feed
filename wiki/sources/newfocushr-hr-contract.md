# New Focus HR Contract — Sole-Source HR Consulting & Employee Handbook

**Raw file:** `raw/research/contract-map/packets/2025-06-10-evt368.pdf` (pp. 110–126); full verbatim text extract at `raw/research/contract-map/newfocushr-source-documents.txt`
**Type:** Staff report + Proposal/Agreement for Services + Terms & Conditions + sole-source justification email chain
**Staff report date:** June 10, 2025
**Submitted by:** Samira Vetter, Town Clerk
**Town signatory on the agreement:** Stefen Wynn, Town Administrator & Treasurer
**Vendor:** New Focus HR, LLC — Kristen Deutsch, M.B.A., CCP, President — Noblesville, Indiana
**Board action:** Approved by the Board of Trustees, June 2025

---

## What the Town agreed to

A "Complete Redesign and Development of a Legally Compliant Employee Handbook and Continuing Human Resources Consultation Services." The agreement also states that New Focus HR "will act as the outsourced human resources consulting company for the Town of Paonia."

| Item | Amount |
|---|---|
| Employee handbook (one-time, fixed fee) | $7,500.00 |
| Annual handbook-update subscription | $150.00 / year |
| HR consulting services | **$175.00 / hour — open-ended, no cap, "billed only as utilized"** |

The proposal itself describes the engagement, in New Focus HR's own words, as addressing the Town's "various human resources challenges that they are trying to resolve within their organization."

## How the agreement came together — the timeline

The order of events is documented in the packet:

| Date | Event |
|---|---|
| May 21, 2025 | Town Clerk Vetter meets New Focus HR president Kristen Deutsch. |
| May 23, 2025 | New Focus HR emails the proposal — **already signed and dated by the vendor**, with Terms & Conditions "made effective as of May 23, 2025." |
| May 28, 2025 | Vetter emails New Focus HR the Town's eight **"sole source" justification questions** — five days *after* the vendor had already signed and dated the agreement. New Focus HR answers the questions itself. |
| June 2025 | The Board approves the agreement. |

No competitive bid was sought. No competing quote from any other firm appears in the packet.

## The "sole source justification"

The Town's purchasing process calls for a justification when a contract is awarded without competition. The justification in this packet is an email exchange. On May 28, 2025, the Town Clerk sent New Focus HR eight questions ("Here are the Sole proprietor questions"); New Focus HR — the vendor — wrote the answers and returned them. Three of the eight answers are "Not applicable." The substance of the answers is that New Focus HR is an experienced HR firm — not that it was the only firm capable of the work.

## Governing law

The Terms & Conditions, §21: *"This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Indiana in Hamilton County, Indiana."* The agreement also specifies, under Logistics, that *"all work will be conducted in the New Focus HR offices"* — in Noblesville, Indiana. New Focus HR's six references in the proposal are all Indiana municipalities (Greencastle, Sheridan, Winchester, Cicero, Ligonier, Bluffton).

One bi-weekly disbursement register — for the Sept 9, 2025 meeting — was not included in its packet, so a small additional amount in that single period cannot be confirmed or ruled out.

## The deliverable

The Town's own staff status reports, carried in successive board packets, show the employee handbook draft was **"complete" by October 2025** and then **"under review by the Town Administrator & Treasurer"** — still listed that way in packets through February 2026. A review of every Town of Paonia board packet from January 2024 through May 2026 found that the finished handbook **has not been brought to the Board for adoption, and has not been made public.**
