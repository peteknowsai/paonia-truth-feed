- **Context:** Follow-up post after the Board published its response without the edits it voted to make

## Key Facts

- Board voted on Sunday (2026-03-30) to edit their response and remove personal attacks before publishing
- Published the response without changing a word: "erroneous and libelous," "smear campaign," "ill-willed players," and "deliberately damaging" all remained
- Vote breakdown on publishing the statement: 5-1
  - **For:** [[mayor-smith]] (Smith), Mejorado, Czech, [[lucy-hunter]] (Hunter), Heck, Tarnow
  - **Against:** Stelter
- Neptune Beach firing not denied by the Board's rebuttal or by Lucy Hunter's separate flyer
- McCarthy sent a formal demand for retraction
- Threatens legal action against each board member who voted yes if "libelous" is not removed and corrected
- Meeting video linked: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=DEe3Jw6u4VE

## Notable Quotes

> "They voted to do one thing and did another. On camera. The meeting is on YouTube. Go watch it."

> "Their own rebuttal doesn't deny the firing. Lucy Hunter's flyer doesn't deny the firing. Their published 'Statement of Fact' doesn't deny the firing. They just call it 'libelous' and hope you won't look it up."

> "Every board member who voted to publish a statement calling my sourced, factual reporting 'libelous' put their name on a lie."

> "I will not be libeled by this body. I have sent a formal demand for retraction."
