# CORA C 26-29: The March 30 Letter Drafts

**Raw files:** `raw/documents/cora-responses/c26-29/` (a "C 26-29 Response" cover letter plus the produced `.eml` threads, `.docx` drafts, redacted PDFs, and screenshots)
**Produced by:** Town Clerk [[samira-vetter|Samira Vetter]], 2026-06-19
**What it is:** The drill-down re-request filed after [[cora-c26-11-libelous-communications-response|C 26-11]] produced only four emails and no drafts. C 26-29 named eight specific items from the Town's own Microsoft 365 records (draft filenames, email subjects, and a Planner task). It was filed June 1, extended to June 18, and produced **June 19, one day after the extended deadline.**

## What was produced

The production contains the drafting record behind the Board's March 30, 2026 response to the "10 Things" flyer:

- **Drafts of the Mayor's response letter** — `Paige draft response to 10 things.docx`, and revisions `Paige draft response to 10 things-LH.docx` and `Paige draft response to 10 things_sw.docx`. The filename initials are LH ([[lucy-hunter|Lucy Hunter]]) and sw ([[stefen-wynn|Stefen Wynn]]).
- **Drafts of the Mayor's report** for the March 30 special meeting — `DRAFT Mayor report for 3.30.26 special meeting.docx` and `..._LH.docx`.
- **The email threads** circulating those drafts: `draft "letter from Trustee"` (and its `Re:` iterations) and `Please see the attached draft Mayor's report` (and its `Re:`/`Fw:` iterations).
- **Redacted PDFs** of the No Kings rally / "Bullying and Harassment" forwards, the `No Kings Rally … Fear for Safety` email with its inline images, and four "Read:" receipts.
- Four screenshots dated 2026-06-18.

## Significance

- **It confirms the C 26-11 production was incomplete.** These drafts existed in the Town's records and were producible. C 26-11 (April) did not produce them, did not log them as withheld, and did not certify them non-existent. The same Town produced them under C 26-29 with no exemption cited for the earlier omission. See [[the-clerks-cora-record]] and [[pattern-of-cora-obstruction]].
- **The drafting record shows who shaped the "libelous" letter.** The Mayor's response to the flyer was drafted and revised in files carrying [[lucy-hunter|Hunter]]'s and [[stefen-wynn|Wynn]]'s initials, circulated before the [[2026-03-30-special-meeting|March 30 special meeting]]. (The Town's public framing of the flyer's authorship is addressed separately in [[they-knew-it-was-mccarthy]].)
- **Timing.** The extension notice was emailed June 10, the day after the original June 9 deadline; the production landed June 19, the day after the extended June 18 deadline.
