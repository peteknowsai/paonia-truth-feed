## What the resume omits

The chronological list presents an unbroken management career, but it does not note the circumstances of two departures that are documented in the public record:

- The Trinidad city manager role (2017–2019) ended with a **resignation to avoid termination** on September 5, 2019. [[greg-sund]]
- The Walsenburg interim role (2020) ended with an **on-the-spot resignation** during a December 2, 2020 council meeting after the council declined to extend his contract. [[greg-sund]]

It also frames the post-2019 period as a series of senior appointments without noting that **every position since 2019 has been interim/short-term**, none converting to a permanent post (he was a finalist but not selected at Telluride in 2022 and Silt in 2024). [[greg-sund]]
