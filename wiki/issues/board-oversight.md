# Board Oversight and Accountability

Whether the Board of Trustees has a functioning process for receiving, investigating, and acting on complaints about town staff, and whether it exercises oversight of the Town Administrator.

## Timeline

- **2024-08-15**: Board removed elected [[bill-brunner|Trustee Brunner]] by 4-1 vote after [[stefen-wynn|Town Administrator Wynn]] submitted conditional resignation. [[2024-08-15-brunner-removal|event]]
- **2024-11-18**: Pete McCarthy sent formal complaint to all board members documenting statutory violations and retaliatory conduct by Wynn. Zero replies. [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]
- **2024-12-02**: Pete resigned from Planning Commission citing board's silence. Zero replies. [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]
- **~2025**: [[cory-heiniger|Cory Heiniger]] resigned as Public Works Director with accusations against Wynn. Board did not investigate. [[board-letter-2026-03-30|source]]
- **2025-10-09**: [[kaja-bowman|Kaja Bowman]] terminated 23 days after inquiring about the complaint process. Board's role unclear. [[2025-10-09-bowman-termination|event]]
- **2026-03-30**: Board called special meeting within 5 days to respond to a citizen's flyer; the same board took no action on a formal complaint for 16 months. [[2026-03-30-special-meeting|event]]

## Current Status

As of 2026-04-05, there is no public evidence that the board has a functioning process for receiving and acting on complaints about the Town Administrator. Three formal complaints/resignations with accusations against Wynn are documented; none received a response or investigation.
