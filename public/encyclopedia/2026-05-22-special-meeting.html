Why this meeting was called

Town Administrator Stefen Wynn's three-year contract is not being renewed. The Town's finance memo dated May 19, 2026 carries the subject line "Town Administrator terms of nonrenewal" and projects his compensation running through mid-July 2026. The Board called a special meeting to handle the gap.

This follows the Board's early-May vote to pause Wynn's reappointment for 30 days, during which residents publicly questioned whether the combined Administrator/Treasurer role should continue in its current form.

Agenda

Single substantive item:

Discussion and Consideration of Hiring an Interim Administrator and the Process for Hiring a Full-Time Administrator

What the packet proposes

  • An interim administrator to bridge the transition.
  • A process for the permanent hire — the packet attaches the City of Florence, CO 2022 City Manager search RFP as a template, signaling intent to run a conventional national executive-search process via a hired consulting firm.
  • The finance memo names no cost figure for the search firm.

See the full packet for details.

Citizen response

How Paonia Should Hire Its Next Administrator — a citizen proposal published the same day — argues the Board should (1) disclose the search-firm cost before voting, (2) weigh growing and training a local hire against a national search, (3) seat residents on the hiring committee, and (4) use the search as the moment to unbundle the over-concentrated Administrator/Treasurer role.
