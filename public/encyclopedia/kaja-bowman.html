Kaja Bowman

From Paoniapedia, the free encyclopedia of the Town of Paonia
Kaja Bowman
Person
RoleDeputy Treasurer, Town of Paonia (former)
Terminated2025-10-09
Record
TypePerson
First recorded2026-04-05
Topicsbowman deputy-treasurer termination retaliation

Timeline

  • 2025-09-16: At the request of several town employees, Bowman approached Mayor Smith to ask whether there was a process for making a complaint to the Board involving the Town Administrator and HR. Bowman had not stated she was making a complaint. source
  • 2025-09-16 (after): Mayor Smith contacted Stefen Wynn, identified Bowman by name, and characterized the inquiry as a complaint before Bowman ever said it was one. source
  • 2025-10-09: Bowman was terminated. 23 days after the inquiry. source
  • 2026-03-28: Wynn emailed Police Chief Laiminger asking how to "begin seeking a protection order from Mr. McCarthy and his accomplices, namely Kaja Bowman," stating she "was seen handing this flyer out." He noted he had "terminated her employment with the Town in October 2025" and that "she is also my lot line sharing neighbor." No police action followed. event source

Additional Context

  • Bowman observed Wynn and town staff (including Town Clerk Vetter) openly expressing negative, inappropriate, and disparaging views about Pete McCarthy in the town offices on numerous occasions during his tenure on the Planning Commission. source
  • Bowman's name is public record (permission confirmed).
