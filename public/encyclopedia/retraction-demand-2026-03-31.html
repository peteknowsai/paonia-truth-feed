Summary

Formal demand that the Board retract its characterization of Pete McCarthy's statements as "erroneous and libelous," issued through a published Board statement. The letter argues the Board's own actions contradict its claim and cites independent reporting to establish the underlying facts.

Key Facts

  • Demands retraction of the phrase "erroneous and libelous" from a published Board statement
  • The claims characterized as libelous: that Stefen Wynn was fired unanimously by Neptune Beach in January 2023 for "flagrant neglect of duty" and "willful misconduct"
  • Cites 6 independent news sources confirming the factual basis of those claims
  • The Board's own published response does not dispute that the firing occurred
  • Lucy Hunter's campaign flyer does not dispute the firing
  • The Board voted 5-1 at the Special Meeting - Board Response to 10 Things Flyer to publish the statement
  • Board members discussed editing the statement but ultimately published it unedited
  • Three specific demands: retraction of the statement, a correction acknowledging the facts are true, and removal of the word "libelous" from all platforms
  • Sets a 10 business day deadline for compliance
  • Reserves rights under Colorado law

Legal Theories Referenced

  • Defamation
  • Defamation per se
  • Section 1983 (civil rights violation under color of law)
  • Anti-SLAPP
