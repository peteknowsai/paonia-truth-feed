CORA C 26-29: The March 30 Letter Drafts

From Paoniapedia, the free encyclopedia of the Town of Paonia
CORA C 26-29: The March 30 Letter Drafts
Source document
Produced byTown Clerk Samira Vetter, 2026-06-19
What it isThe drill-down re-request filed after C 26-11 produced only four emails and no drafts. C 26-29 named eight specific items from the Town's own Microsoft 365 records (draft filenames, email subjects, and a Planner task). It was filed June 1, extended to June 18, and produced June 19, one day after the extended deadline.

What was produced

The production contains the drafting record behind the Board's March 30, 2026 response to the "10 Things" flyer:

  • Drafts of the Mayor's response letter — Paige draft response to 10 things.docx, and revisions Paige draft response to 10 things-LH.docx and Paige draft response to 10 things_sw.docx. The filename initials are LH (Lucy Hunter) and sw (Stefen Wynn).
  • Drafts of the Mayor's report for the March 30 special meeting — DRAFT Mayor report for 3.30.26 special meeting.docx and ..._LH.docx.
  • The email threads circulating those drafts: draft "letter from Trustee" (and its Re: iterations) and Please see the attached draft Mayor's report (and its Re:/Fw: iterations).
  • Redacted PDFs of the No Kings rally / "Bullying and Harassment" forwards, the No Kings Rally … Fear for Safety email with its inline images, and four "Read:" receipts.
  • Four screenshots dated 2026-06-18.

Significance

  • It confirms the C 26-11 production was incomplete. These drafts existed in the Town's records and were producible. C 26-11 (April) did not produce them, did not log them as withheld, and did not certify them non-existent. The same Town produced them under C 26-29 with no exemption cited for the earlier omission. See The Clerk's CORA Record and Pattern of CORA Obstruction.
  • The drafting record shows who shaped the "libelous" letter. The Mayor's response to the flyer was drafted and revised in files carrying Hunter's and Wynn's initials, circulated before the March 30 special meeting. (The Town's public framing of the flyer's authorship is addressed separately in They Knew It Was McCarthy — Then Called Him “Anonymous”.)
  • Timing. The extension notice was emailed June 10, the day after the original June 9 deadline; the production landed June 19, the day after the extended June 18 deadline.
