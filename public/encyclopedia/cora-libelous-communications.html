Summary

A CORA request filed by Pete McCarthy on 2026-04-06 seeking three categories of records related to the Board's characterization of his flyer as "libelous."

Records Requested

  1. All communications (email, text messages, Microsoft Teams messages, and written correspondence) between any combination of Mayor Paige Smith, Town Administrator Stefen Wynn, Town Attorney Clayton Buchner, and any member of the Board of Trustees, regarding Pete McCarthy, Pete McCarthy's flyer, or the Board's written response to that flyer, for the period of March 25 through March 30, 2026.

  2. All drafts, revisions, and tracked changes of the letter published by the Board of Trustees on or about March 30, 2026, responding to Pete McCarthy's flyer, created prior to or on March 30, 2026.

  3. All legal opinions, memoranda, or written advice from Town Attorney Clayton Buchner regarding the use of the terms "libelous," "libel," or "defamation" in connection with Pete McCarthy, his flyer, or any citizen communication, for the period of March 25 through March 30, 2026.

Significance

This request targets the decision-making process behind calling Pete's flyer "libelous." If the Town Attorney advised against using that word and it was used anyway, that changes the analysis. If he advised in favor of it, that raises questions about the legal advice. Either way, the communications would reveal how the Board's response was developed and who drove the characterization.
