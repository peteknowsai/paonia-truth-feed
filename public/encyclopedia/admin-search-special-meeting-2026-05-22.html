Finance Memo (packet pp. 5)

From: Ashley Rodarte, Payroll and Accounting Specialist To: The Board of Trustees Date: May 19, 2026 Subject (verbatim): "Town Administrator terms of nonrenewal"

The Town's own document uses the word nonrenewal — not "resignation." Wynn was hired in 2023 on a three-year contract; the memo projects his compensation running through mid-July 2026.

  • The administrator's salary is split across four funds — 25% Admin, 35% Water, 35% Sewer, 5% Trash. This is the combined Town Administrator & Treasurer position; see "Controls all the money, alone": what the evidence actually shows.

Executive search funding

The memo proposes that an Executive Search Consulting firm be paid out of the "Legal, Engineering and Professional Services" budget line across the same four funds. Line-item room as of April 30:

The memo states no dollar figure for the search firm itself. The Board is being asked to consider a consultant contract whose price is not in the packet.

RFP Template (packet pp. 6–8)

The packet attaches a City of Florence, Colorado "Request for Proposal for Recruitment Services" (City Manager search, 2022). It is watermarked "EXAMPLE" — a template, not a Paonia document. It indicates the Board intends to model its own search RFP on a conventional national executive-search process.

Notable in the Florence template: the hiring committee is "2 Council and 3 Staff" — zero citizen seats. See How Paonia Should Hire Its Next Administrator for the case that Paonia's committee should include residents.
