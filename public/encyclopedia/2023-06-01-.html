Paonia Municipal Court -- June 1, 2023

Presiding: Municipal Court Judge (name unclear from captions) Cases heard: 2

Case 1: People v. Willow Man (Case P23-0154)

  • Charge: Disregarding a stop sign (4-point traffic violation)
  • Penalty: $80 fine + $25 court costs (set by statute)
  • Plea: Guilty
  • Outcome: Fine imposed as stated
  • Notable exchange: Defendant came to court hoping to get points dropped from her license. Judge explained he has no authority over points -- that's handled by the state. Advised that prepaying a ticket within 20 days of issuance reduces points through a state process. Defendant noted police officers didn't tell her this; judge agreed they should have.

Case 2: People v. [Alina Dash] (name approximate)

  • Charge: Shoplifting (1 count), maximum penalty $2,650
  • Plea: Guilty
  • Prosecution input: Sgt. Henderson deferred to the judge on sentencing. Confirmed stolen items were recovered the same day. Noted additional theft investigations are ongoing but not relevant to this case.
  • Defendant's statement: Explained she stole food because her husband was not working and she couldn't afford groceries despite seeking overtime and trying to get him hired.
  • Sentence: $75 fine + $25 court costs ($100 total), with payment plan available
  • Judge's closing: "I hope things are getting better for you."

Notable Quotes
