STR Citizen Initiative Rejected

From Paoniapedia, the free encyclopedia of the Town of Paonia

Timeline

  • 2025-05-21: Pete McCarthy submitted a citizen initiative petition regarding short-term rental regulations.
  • 2025-05-28: Town Clerk Samira Vetter rejected the petition under C.R.S. 31-11-106(1). The rejection lacked the specificity required by law.
  • ~2026-03: Town Attorney Buchner's detailed legal analysis of the petition, which existed at the time of rejection, was finally released publicly. Nearly ten months after rejection.

What the Record Shows

  • The petitioner was not given the opportunity to review the legal reasoning, respond, or amend the petition before rejection.
  • The Town Attorney's analysis was withheld for nearly ten months.
  • This was the first of two initiative rejections by Vetter. The second was the surveillance camera initiative on 2026-04-03.
