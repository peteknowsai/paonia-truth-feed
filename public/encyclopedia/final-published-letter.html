Summary

The edited, final version of the Board's response letter. Published after the March 30 special meeting. The Board voted to remove personal attacks from the original draft before publication.

Changes from Original Draft

The following language was removed or softened in the final version:

Original Draft Final Version
"the anonymous author(s) penned a list of misleading and false accusations...deliberately damaging and inaccurate attempted 'gotchas'" "A posting was made that we feel contained false and misleading accusations"
"Erroneous and libelous statements were made" "Statements were made"
"attempted to offer a spin...to paint the Town Administrator in a negative light" Removed
"a few ill-willed players in Town harassing, bullying, and defaming Town Administrators" Removed
"this smear campaign" Removed
"deliberately damaging misinformation" Removed

What Was NOT Changed

The substantive rebuttals on each topic (governance, grants, staffing, finances, CIP, trees, cameras) are identical between draft and final versions. The closing statement is identical: "the Town of Paonia would not be in the enviable position it's in today" without the Administrator.

Publication Sequence

Per the Facebook posts:

  1. Board voted on 2026-03-30 to edit the statement before publication
  2. The unedited original was published first
  3. The edited version replaced it at some point after
  4. Both versions exist in the public record
