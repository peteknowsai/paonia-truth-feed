Summary

Cover letter for revised surveillance camera prohibition initiative. The petition has been revised from the prior rejected submission to remove the provisions Vetter identified as potentially administrative or judicial (contract restrictions, citizen enforcement, voter approval for amendment). What remains is the core policy prohibition with standard structural provisions.

Requests confirmation of receipt and acceptance within 5 business days per C.R.S. 31-11-106.

Significance

This is the third initiative submission related to surveillance. The approach narrows the initiative to remove objectionable provisions while preserving the core policy question: should the town surveil citizens with cameras on public property?
