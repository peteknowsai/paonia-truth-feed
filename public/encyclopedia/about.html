About Paoniapedia

From Paoniapedia, the free encyclopedia of the Town of Paonia

Paoniapedia is a Wikipedia-style rendering of the Paonia civic-transparency wiki — the same source material behind paoniatruth.site, presented as a browsable encyclopedia.

Every article is generated from a plain-text wiki maintained by residents. Facts are sourced to the Town of Paonia's own records: budgets, meeting minutes, CORA responses, and court filings. Links in red point to topics not yet written up.

It currently contains 202 articles across 6 categories.
