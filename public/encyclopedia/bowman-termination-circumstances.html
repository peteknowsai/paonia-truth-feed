What Were the Circumstances of Bowman's Termination?

From Paoniapedia, the free encyclopedia of the Town of Paonia
What Were the Circumstances of Bowman's Termination?
Open question
QuestionWhat was the stated basis for Kaja Bowman's termination on 2025-10-09, and was it connected to her inquiry about the complaint process on 2025-09-16?
Record
TypeOpen question
First recorded2026-04-05
Topicsbowman termination smith wynn

What the Record Shows

  • Bowman approached Mayor Smith on 2025-09-16 at the request of several town employees to ask about the complaint process.
  • The mayor contacted Wynn, identified Bowman by name, and characterized the inquiry as a complaint.
  • Bowman was terminated 23 days later.
  • The board complaint letter requested "a review of the circumstances surrounding Ms. Bowman's termination, including the Mayor's role in disclosing the employee's identity to Mr. Wynn."

What Could Answer This

  • Bowman's termination letter or documentation
  • Any board discussion of the termination (meeting minutes)
  • HR records related to the termination decision
  • CORA request for communications between the mayor, Wynn, and HR in the Sept-Oct 2025 period

Status

Open. No public documentation of the termination basis has been identified.
