The Case Against Mayor Smith

A recall is the most direct tool residents have when the people in office stop answering to them. The recall now circulating is aimed at one official in particular — Mayor Paige Smith — and the reason is not a single decision anyone could chalk up to a bad day. It is a pattern, documented in the Town's own records, of using the power of the office against the residents it was meant to serve. Every fact below comes from public documents.
