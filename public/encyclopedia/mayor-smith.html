Key Actions

Brunner removal (2024-08)

  • Brought the charges against Trustee Brunner and served as prosecutor at the removal hearing. Could not vote. source

Bowman disclosure (2025-09)

  • After Kaja Bowman approached the mayor on 2025-09-16 to ask whether there was a process for making a complaint about the Town Administrator and HR, Mayor Smith contacted Stefen Wynn, identified Bowman by name, and characterized the inquiry as a complaint. Bowman had not stated she was making a complaint. Bowman was terminated 23 days later. source

Special meeting (2026-03-30)

  • Mayor's agenda report called Pete's 10 Things flyer "libelous." source
  • Proposed spending taxpayer money on paid display ads in three newspapers (Merchant Herald, High Country Shopper, Delta County Independent) to respond to the flyer. source
  • Report referred to the "anonymous author(s)" despite the board being aware Pete had publicly claimed the flyer. source
  • Stated concern that public criticism "can place us in jeopardy with our current granting and lending agencies." source

Retraction demand (2026-03-31)

  • Pete sent a formal demand for retraction to Mayor Smith and all board members. source
  • Demanded retraction and correction within 10 business days.
  • Reserved rights under Colorado law including defamation, Section 1983, and anti-SLAPP.

Public statements

  • Has stated publicly that her most important duty is to "protect the town staff." source
  • Facebook post #3 calls for her resignation based on the pattern: disclosed Bowman, ignored McCarthy's complaint, ignored Heiniger's accusations, then used taxpayer resources to label citizen criticism "libelous."

Voting Record

  • 2026-03-30: Voted to publish the board's response calling Pete's flyer "libelous." (5-1, for publication) source
