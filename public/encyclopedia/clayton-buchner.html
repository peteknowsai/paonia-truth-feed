Initiative legal analysis

  • Authored a detailed legal memorandum analyzing Pete McCarthy's STR citizen initiative petition, addressed to the Board of Trustees. The analysis existed at the time of the initiative's rejection (2025-05-28) but was not provided to the petitioner. It was released publicly approximately 2026-03, nearly ten months after rejection. source

Counter-flyer coordination

  • Coordinated with Stefen Wynn and Trustee Hunter on Hunter's unsigned counter-flyer responding to Pete's 10 Things flyer (2026-03-26). Per the mayor's agenda report. source
