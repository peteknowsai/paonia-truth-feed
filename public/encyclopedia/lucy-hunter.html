From Paoniapedia, the free encyclopedia of the Town of Paonia
Lucy Hunter
Person
RoleTrustee, Town of Paonia
StatusCurrent (as of 2026-04-05)
Record
TypePerson
First recorded2026-04-05
Topicshunter trustee board

Voting Record

  • 2024-08-15: Voted to remove Trustee Brunner. (4-1 vote, for removal) source
  • 2026-03-30: Voted to publish the board's response calling Pete's flyer "libelous." (5-1 vote, for publication) source

Key Actions

Counter-flyer (2026-03-26)

  • Created a counter-flyer responding to Pete's 10 Things flyer, posted throughout town unsigned within 24 hours of the flyer's distribution. source
  • Per the mayor's agenda report, the counter-flyer was coordinated with Town Administrator Wynn and Town Attorney Buchner. source

Response to formal complaint (2026-03-31)

  • Only board member to respond to Pete's formal complaint letter. source
  • Wrote in individual capacity, not on behalf of the Board.
  • Acknowledged the complaint and stated she takes its contents seriously.
  • Could not address matters involving pending litigation or personnel affairs.
  • Agreed that a complaint process should exist: "A municipality should have a clear, documented, and accessible process that ensures complaints of this nature are received by the Board directly, not routed through the subject of the complaint."
  • Stated she intends to raise the complaint process as a matter requiring the Board's attention.

Discrepancy

  • Hunter's rebuttal flyer was unsigned, yet the mayor's report treated it as a legitimate, authorized response. Pete's sourced, publicly claimed flyer was characterized as "libelous." source
