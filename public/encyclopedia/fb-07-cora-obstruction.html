Key Facts

  • C 26-09 (Verkada): $167.90 charged for a one-click export. 57,460 audit rows with user IDs, IPs, device IDs, and serial numbers redacted under "security arrangements."
  • C 26-11 (libelous communications): Four emails produced for a six-day window covering a special meeting and a jointly-signed Board letter. Two of the four are Pete's own. No scheduling emails for the meeting. No drafts of the letter. No replies from six of the seven trustees. All Buchner advice withheld under attorney-client privilege with no privilege log.
  • C 26-12 (M365 audit log): The "March 2026" file is a byte-identical duplicate of the April 2026 file (verified by matching RecordIds). Every monthly file is capped at 50,000 rows (the Purview UI export cap). The AuditData column is 100% redacted across all files. The April file stops at April 9. Earliest data is Nov 1, 2025 even though 180-day retention should reach back to Oct 25.
  • The Town is on Microsoft's 180-day retention — the minimum tier Microsoft sells. The 2024 M365 denial Pete refiled in September 2024 was confirmed improper by CFOIC; those records have now aged out permanently.
  • Brunner precedent: Judge Steven Schultz already ruled the Town "failed to exercise reasonable diligence or reasonable inquiry" in a prior CORA case and awarded legal costs. The Town's practices have not changed.

Notable Quotes

"The file labeled 'March 2026' is a duplicate of the April 2026 file. I verified this by matching record IDs."

"They waited the full 15 working days. Here is the entire production: 1. My own email to the Board. 2. The Mayor forwarding my email to the Town Administrator, 8 minutes later. 3. Lucy Hunter telling the Mayor on March 26 that the flyer was hers. 4. Lucy Hunter's individual-capacity reply to me on March 30. That is it. Four emails. Two of them are my own."

"This is what obstruction looks like when it is not loud. It is quiet. It is a duplicate file. It is a column of [REDACTED]. It is four emails where there should be forty."

"A board that coordinates a special meeting about a citizen and produces zero emails about it is a board that knows their communications would not survive daylight."
