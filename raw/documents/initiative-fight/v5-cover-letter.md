Re: Submission of Citizen Initiative Petition, "Paonia Surveillance Camera Prohibition Ordinance"

Dear Ms. Vetter:

I hereby submit the attached citizen initiative petition for the "Paonia Surveillance Camera Prohibition Ordinance" pursuant to C.R.S. 31-11-106.

The initiative addresses a single subject: prohibiting government surveillance cameras on public property. It contains seven sections: a title, a prohibition, definitions, three narrow exceptions, a removal timeline for existing cameras, a severability clause, and an effective date.

This petition has been revised from the prior submission to address the concerns raised in your April 3, 2026 rejection letter. Specifically, the provisions you identified as potentially administrative or judicial in character (contract restrictions, citizen enforcement, and voter approval for amendment) have been removed. What remains is a straightforward policy prohibition with standard structural provisions.

Please confirm receipt and acceptance of the petition for circulation within five (5) business days per C.R.S. 31-11-106.
