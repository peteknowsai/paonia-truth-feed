## SUMMONS

**TO THE ABOVE-NAMED DEFENDANT:**

You are hereby summoned and required to file with the Clerk of this Court an Answer or other Response to the attached Verified Petition for Judicial Review and for Mandamus.

If served within the State of Colorado, you must file your Answer or other Response **within twenty-one (21) days** after this Summons is served on you.

If served outside the State of Colorado or by publication, you must file your Answer or other Response **within thirty-five (35) days** after this Summons is served on you.

If you fail to file your Answer or other Response to the Verified Petition in writing within the applicable time period, judgment by default may be entered against you for the relief demanded in the Petition without further notice.

Note that this is also a Verified Petition under C.R.C.P. 106(a)(4) and (a)(2). The Court may, upon motion, order an expedited briefing schedule and issue a Rule to Show Cause requiring an earlier response.
