Dear Board of Trustees,

I hereby resign my position as Planning Commissioner, effective immediately.

Two weeks have passed since I submitted a detailed documentation of serious
governance issues to this Board. Your complete silence speaks volumes about
the state of oversight in our town.

I take this step only after witnessing the systematic dismantling of
Planning Commission authority and experiencing persistent hostility while
attempting to fulfill my duties. The November 20th work session transcript
clearly documents:

- The Town Administrator threatening to abandon his duties when I asked
basic process questions
- The Mayor and Administrator working in tandem to dismiss legitimate
requests for documentation
- My repeated requests over two months for written policy explanations
being stonewalled
- A pattern of administrative retaliation when commissioners raise concerns

The Planning Commission has been reduced to a ceremonial role, expected to
rubber-stamp decisions without proper review or documentation. When
commissioners attempt to exercise meaningful oversight, they face hostility
and dismissal from town administration.

Your continued silence regarding these serious issues makes clear that
proper oversight is not a priority for this board. I cannot in good
conscience continue serving on a commission where asking questions and
requesting documentation is met with threats and retaliation.

The community deserves better than performative governance. My duty is to
the citizens of this town. Since I can no longer effectively fulfill that
duty in this compromised environment, I must step aside.

For the record,
Pete McCarthy
Former Planning Commissioner
