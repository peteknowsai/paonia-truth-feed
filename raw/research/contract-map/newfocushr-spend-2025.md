## 4. Notes

- **Handbook fee — only the first half appears.** Invoice **#4320** ($3,900.00, dated
  07/29/2025, described "Employee Handbooks half project cost **and sub**") is the
  $3,750 first-half handbook fee **plus** the $150 annual subscription bundled into one
  invoice ($3,750 + $150 = $3,900). The subscription is NOT a separate register line —
  it rode along on #4320.
- **No second $3,750 handbook half** appears in any June–December 2025 register. If it
  was billed, it would land in a 2026 packet (outside this review window) or has not
  yet been invoiced.
- **Everything else is hourly consulting** at the $175/hr rate, billed in small
  invoices: #4325, #4354, #4439 are each $87.50 (= 0.5 hr); #4378 is $1,225.00
  (= 7 hrs); #4398 is $350.00 (= 2 hrs).
- All New Focus HR costs are coded to the **"-20" LEGAL, ENGINEERING & PROFESSIONAL
  SERVICES** GL line and split across Town funds (General/Admin 10-41, PD 10-42,
  Water 60-50, Wastewater 70-51, Sanitation 80-52) — typically 4 or 5 ways.
- **Two packets could not be checked for New Focus HR:**
  - **2025-09-09 (evt371)** — the published 94-page packet PDF contains no
    Payment Approval Report register at all (only Treasurer budget reports). The
    Sept 9 disbursements were approved but the register was not included in the packet.
  - **2025-09-30 (evt491)** — a 31-page special meeting with no consent agenda and
    no disbursements; no register exists for this meeting.
- The phrase "The Town recently contracted with New Focus HR to begin the process of
  updating personnel policies and the employee handbook" appears as a recurring
  **Departmental Scorecard** status line ("Personnel Policy and Handbook Updates") in
  many packets (07-22, 08-26, 09-23, 10-14, 12-09, etc.). That is prose, not a
  disbursement line, and was not counted.
