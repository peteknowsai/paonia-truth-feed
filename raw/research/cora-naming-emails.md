## How to read and respond to this request (universal framing — applies to every numbered item below)

Each item is identified by its **verbatim email subject line**, a **date window**, and the **specific mailboxes** in which the Town's own Microsoft 365 unified audit log records the message. The Town produced that audit log itself; the subjects, timestamps, and mailbox routing below are quoted directly from it.

1. **No "cannot locate" claims.** The Town cannot credibly claim it is unable to find a record whose subject line, date, and mailbox are quoted verbatim from the Town's own M365 audit log. Each subject is a known, indexed mail item. A simple subject search in each named mailbox will return it.

2. **Scope of each item.** For each thread I request: the full **email body** (all messages in the thread, including every RE: and FW: in the chain), all **attachments**, any **draft** versions retained, and the full **transmittal/header data** (From, To, Cc, Bcc, Date/Time, and message-ID).

3. **Native format with headers.** Produce in native electronic format (or as PDF/EML that preserves the complete header block showing all senders and recipients). Do not strip recipients or flatten the chain.

4. **Withholding requires written grounds + a privilege log.** For any record or portion withheld or redacted, provide a written statement of the grounds for denial under **C.R.S. § 24-72-204(4)**, and a **privilege log** identifying for each withheld item: date, author, all recipients, subject, the specific privilege/exemption claimed, and the factual basis for it.

5. **Segregable portions.** Where only part of a record is privileged, the non-privileged portions must be segregated and produced (§ 24-72-205 / § 24-72-204(4)).

6. **What is NOT privileged — do not over-withhold.**
   - **Inbound citizen correspondence** (e.g., my own retraction demand, my CORA requests, citizen complaints) is not the Town's privileged material; a third party's own letter to the government is a public record.
   - **PR / press / messaging coordination, agenda wording, packet assembly, advertising/vendor pricing, and public-meeting documents** are administrative/public records, not legal advice.
   - **Subject lines, dates, participants, and routing** are factual metadata and are producible even where a body is withheld.
   - **Broad, non-attorney distribution waives confidentiality.** A message blasted to all seven trustees, the Police Chief, shared mailboxes, and administrative staff is not a confidential attorney-client communication merely because counsel was copied or someone typed "Attorney Client privilege" into the subject. A self-applied privilege label by a non-attorney over a citizen's inbound letter does not create privilege.

7. **Deletions.** Where the audit log shows SoftDelete, MoveToDeletedItems, or HardDelete operations on a requested item, produce the record from backup/retention/journaling and identify the deletion (who, when). Spoliation of public records is independently actionable.

Items are ordered by evidentiary value: the naming proof and the response-drafting/coordination threads first.
