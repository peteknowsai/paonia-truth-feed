# Paonia Extends STR Moratorium, Selects Facilitator for Ad Hoc Committee

**Author:** Justin Shaw
**Date:** March 25, 2026 (Updated March 31, 2026)
**Source:** Delta County Independent
**URL:** https://www.deltacountyindependent.com/news/paonia-extends-str-moratorium-selects-facilitator-for-ad-hoc-committee/article_0768760f-ef02-4caf-8f81-330930ae2fd3.html

---

The Paonia Board of Trustees unanimously voted to extend the town's short-term rental (STR) moratorium through December 31, 2026, during their March 24 meeting. This marks the third extension since the board initially imposed a 180-day pause on STR enforcement in April 2025.

The board also selected Scott Brown to facilitate the town's ad hoc STR committee. Brown was one of two applicants considered; the other was Dev Carey.

## Key Details

**Moratorium Extension:** Resolution 04-26 extends the pause on STR enforcement after the committee acknowledged it would not complete its work before the existing moratorium expired April 4.

**Committee Work:** Mayor Paige Smith emphasized the need to clarify the committee's original scope, noting that it hasn't been tasked with writing an ordinance or creating a phased approach to regulating short-term rentals.

**Facilitator Selection:** Brown charges $60 per hour, while Carey proposed $100 per hour. When accounting for time commitments, the costs proved comparable, prompting Trustee Wynn to select the more responsive candidate.

**Committee Topics:** The seven original topics assigned to the committee include licensing fees, exterior door dimensions, owner-occupancy requirements, and zoning classifications.
