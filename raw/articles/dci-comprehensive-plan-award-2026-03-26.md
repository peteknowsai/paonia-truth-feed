# Paonia Comprehensive Plan Earns National Planning Award

**Author:** Special to the DCI
**Date:** 2026-03-26
**Source:** DCI
**URL:** https://www.deltacountyindependent.com/news/paonia-comprehensive-plan-earns-national-planning-award/article_a982b96e-094b-4af5-97a5-227aa862c84a.html

---

The town of Paonia's 2025 comprehensive plan has earned a Merit Award from the American Planning Association's Small Town and Rural Division. The plan, titled "A Community Driven Framework for a Resilient Rural Future," was selected under the Vernon Dienes Award for Small Jurisdictions, which recognizes planning excellence in smaller municipalities and rural areas.

The comprehensive plan was developed through collaborative efforts involving Paonia's planning commission, board of trustees, Delta County partners, and community members. Professional planning consultants also supported the process.

The document addresses multiple areas including housing, economic vitality, infrastructure development, and environmental stewardship. According to Town Administrator and Treasurer Stefen Wynn: "This award reflects the dedication of our community, our elected and appointed officials and the professional team that worked together to develop a plan that is both practical and visionary."

Wynn emphasized the plan represents sustained commitment and collaboration necessary to achieve community goals. The award recognizes planning efforts demonstrating innovation, public engagement, and practical implementation potential in smaller jurisdictions.

The formal presentation of this honor will take place at the APA's Small Town and Rural Division awards meeting scheduled for April 24 in Detroit.
