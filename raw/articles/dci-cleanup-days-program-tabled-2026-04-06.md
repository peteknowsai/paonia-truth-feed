# Cleanup days program tabled

**Author:** Justin Shaw | justin.shaw@deltacountyindependent.com
**Date:** 2026-04-06
**Source:** DCI
**URL:** https://www.deltacountyindependent.com/news/cleanup-days-program-tabled-board-opens-seats-on-two-advisory-bodies/article_da0e210e-995c-43e5-a3df-b36147386ed0.html

## 
- 

- 
                                
                                
                                
                                

A proposal to revive Paonia's cleanup days program was shelved for at least another year after the board of trustees voted 3-2 at its March 24 meeting to postpone planning for the program rather than direct staff to develop a proposal this season.

Several trustees and staff participated remotely due to illness, including Trustee Hector Mejorado, Wynn and town clerk Vetter. Mayor Pro Tem Stelzer had an excused absence and the town attorney was also not present.
